The goal of our paper was to create a soccer ball launcher which would orient itself by rotating pitch and yaw to orient the launcher towards a target and launch a ball to hit that target.
This is an interesting project because we have to think about a lot of aspects of sensing, planning, computation and actuation.
We think this is a device that is in demand in most training spaces, and having something like this will greatly benefit atletic training.
